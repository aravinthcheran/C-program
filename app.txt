detailed description of the app donate +:
In the modern era where technology grows at an unravelled pace, there are certain group of people who are in basic need. 
As the quality of life increases, the need to help the needy people also increases.
As the world shift towards a comfortable pace with the help of technology, the need to access of essential components become necessary.
The app donate + is a simple app that helps you to donate your old clothes, books, toys, etc. to the needy people. 
The app is designed in such a way that it is easy to use and can be used by anyone. 
The app is designed to help the trusts or orphanages to get the required items from the people who are willing to donate.
this app ensures that the items are delivered to the right place and to the right people.
